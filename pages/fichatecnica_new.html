Synopsis

In a distant future, humanity has spread across the stars, encountering diverse alien civilizations and uncovering ancient mysteries. The Eternal White follows the crew of the starship Odyssey as they navigate the complexities of interstellar politics, explore uncharted territories, and confront threats that could unravel the fabric of the galaxy.

Cast

Anya Petrova as Captain Eva Rostova, Ben Carter as Dr. Aris Thorne, and Chloe Davis as Lieutenant Commander Kai.

Creators

Created by Elias Vance and Isabella Rossi, a cinematic journey through the cosmos.
